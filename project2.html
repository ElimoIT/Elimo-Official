Project Overview:

ICOAE is a smart medical emergency management system consisting of a smart wrist device mobile application and a web application which connects patients, guardians, medical service offering companies and ambulances in one. This allows patients to connect immediately in an emergency with a single button press through their smart device. This device is capable of tracking a patient’s location, current temperature, pulse and blood oxygen. This allows emergency service personnel to use the web dashboard to inform the nearest available ambulance and the ambulance driver can track the location of the patient through a mobile application.
